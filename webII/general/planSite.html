Planning a Website

Plans Due Date: Friday, 5/28/21

Think, Brainstorm, Plan, Build

Communicate to the Audience: the main purpose of the site

The Audience:

  • The reason the website exists
  • Who are they?
  • What is their age group?
  • What are their special interests?
  • What is their assumed prior knowledge?
  • What does the audience need to know that the website will provide?
  • How can the site design fit the audience?

The plan:

  • (Please finish and submit the plans before writing any code)
  • Think, brainstorm.
  • Submit a 1 page (300 word minimum) explanation/description of the audience (see 7 points, above), the website, and details of how the website is appropriate for the audience. Include a complete list of style ideas.
  • Submit sketches for the site, one sketch for every unique page layout design
  • Use Google Slides or another method to make the sketches of the page layout designs.

*If you start coding before the planning is over, you will lose 10% of the project's points.
