Self Evaluation of a Website Project

Due Date: optional

Complete this self evaluation after you have finished a website project

Write a narrative self evaluation of your work on this project. Save it as a word file, in the same folder as your project. Email me when done, giving me the name of the file I should look at. Consider these questions:

  1. Explain how your work meets the requirements for the project
  2. To what degree did you meet your goals for this project? What were your successes?
  3. What you learned about web design as a result of this project?
  4. What are the project's strengths?
  5. What are some parts of the project you think could be improved?
  6. Give yourself a grade.
