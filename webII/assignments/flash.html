Beginning Flash Tutorials

To begin to learn Flash, you will work your way through some preliminary lessons and a tutorial. These will cover the following:

  • Getting Started with Flash
  • Illustrating in Flash
  • Adding and Editing Text
  • Creating and Editing Symbols
  • Understanding Layers
  • Creating Tweened Animation
  • Creating Buttons
  • Begin by copying the Flash MX folder from G:\HS\Student\Web Design II and pasting it into your web design folder. There are files in the Flash folder that you will use during the lessons and the tutorial.
  • Open the Flash program from the Novell Delivered Applications window.
  • Next select the Help menu and then select Lessons.
  • You will see the Flash MX lessons listed down the right side of the window that opens. Begin with the Getting Started with Flash lesson and work your way through the Creating Buttons lesson.
  • After completing the lessons, go back to the help menu and select Tutorial. Work your way through the Introduction to Flash MX tutorial.

These lessons and the tutorial will give you some basic understanding of how Flash works. Try to make an animation to include in your next website.
