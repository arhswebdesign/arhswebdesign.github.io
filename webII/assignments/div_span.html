Semantic Elements

Due Date: Monday, 4/13/20

In this assignment you will begin to use the new html5 semantic elements, as well as the div and span tags. These tags are used to group and structure a web document.

Until recently, many advanced web pages were mostly made up of <div> elements, and they were distinguished using classes or ids.


In html5, new elements have been introduced.  This saves time when styling, but also helps search engines learn more about your page's content and structure.
