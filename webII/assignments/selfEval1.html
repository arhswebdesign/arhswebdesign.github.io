Self Evaluation of the Digital Portfolio

Complete this self evaluation after you have finished the digital portfolio project

Write a narrative self evaluation of your digital portfolio project. Email me the writing in a word file. Consider these questions:

  1. Explain how your work meets the requirements for the project
  2. To what degree did you meet your goals for this project? What were your successes?
  3. What have you reviewed from your earlier web design study and what you learned about web design as a result of this project?
  4. What are the project's strengths?
  5. What are some parts of the project you think could be improved?
  6. Give yourself a grade.
