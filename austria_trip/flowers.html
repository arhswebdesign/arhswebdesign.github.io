Wildflowers of the Austrian Alps

There are many beautiful wildflowers to see in the Alps.

  • Wildflowers
  • Gentians
  • Pinks
  • Orchids
  • Mints

As a scarce, short-lived flower found in remote mountain areas, the plant has been used as a symbol for alpinism, for rugged beauty and purity associated with the Alps, and as a national symbol. According to folk tradition, giving this flower to a loved one is a promise of dedication.
