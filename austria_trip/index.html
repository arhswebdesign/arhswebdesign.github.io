Venice, Italy

Venice is a small italian city, known for it's beauty and complexity. It has inspired writers for centuries and the many stories set in Venice only add to its mystique.

It has been a city since Roman times and has been an important hub of international trade for over a thousand years.

It connected Europe to the silk and spice route through Central Asia

14th century Venice was the most prosperous city in all of Europe.
