Web Site Evaluation: Your Own List

Web Design recommendations

 Be organized

 Simple, but not too simple

 Easy to use - navigation

 Lots of relevant information that achieves the purpose of the site

 Just because you can doesn’t mean you should

 Consistency – theme and layout

 Don’t want distractions – busy websites are bad

 Not “trying too hard”

 Appropriate font

 Information about the website is up front – make it clear what it’s about

 Clear and common theme

 Relevant graphics

 Working links
